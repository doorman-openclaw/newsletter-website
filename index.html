Questions?

How does it work?

Newsletter connects to your existing email (Gmail or Outlook) via IMAP, automatically detects newsletters, and gives you a clean reader view. Every action syncs back to your inbox ‚Äî archive in Newsletter, it's archived in Gmail.

What email providers are supported?

Gmail and Outlook via OAuth 2.0. Any IMAP-compatible email service will work.

Is my data private?

Yes. All email content is processed locally on your Mac. Nothing is sent to our servers. We don't track usage, collect analytics, or sync data to the cloud.

Can I use this on multiple Macs?

Yes. One license works on all your Macs. Your read state is synced through your email provider (via IMAP flags), so marking something as read on one Mac updates it everywhere.
